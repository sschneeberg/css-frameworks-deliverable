Potato Chips Lite+

A fresh summer take on the classic potato chip

Potato Chips Lite-

This summer enjoy fresh, crisp potato chips made at home!

It's just so simple:

Thinly slice your potatoes and lay to dry on a paper towel.

Pat each side and enjoy with your favorite dips!

Skip the salt to save your blood pressure!!

Just Like Chicken+

A vegan replacement to die for

Just Like Chicken-

Gone vegan before the neighborhood barbeque? Try this one out!

Simply take your potatoes and peel completely

Shape the potato into that classic chicken brest profile

Coat in your favorite barbeque sauce and toss on the grill!

Just delicious, just like chicken!

Christmas in July+

Bring the holiday joy straight to the summer

Chridtmas in July-

Crave that Christmas baked potato?

With this simple trick you can have your comfort food classic and beat the heat!

Peel your potatoes completely and set in salt water

Bake to perfection and then immediately freeze

Enjoy your holiday treat frozen in time!
